About TAXI!

TAXI! was an ambitious project created for the purpose of serving the citizens of India with the best possible online cab booking services that currently offers rentals and outstations. Its true goal is to create a portal for users to book cabs without any hassle faced in the common call-taxi and pre-paid taxi systems.

TAXI! is actually a project created by Computer Science and Engineering students at GITAM Institute of Technology that is needed to be submitted to the Web Technologies Laboratory as a case study. This is the honest reason why this website has been designed in its first place.
